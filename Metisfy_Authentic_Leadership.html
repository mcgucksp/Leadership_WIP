This assessment maps where you stand across the 11 components of authentic leadership. It will show you your strengths, your growth areas, and where to focus next.

A note before you start

This assessment works best when you answer based on what is actually true for you right now, not what you aspire to be or what you think a good leader should say.

There are no right answers here. The value is in the honest ones.

If you find yourself wanting to score highly on everything, pause. The most useful thing this assessment can do is show you where you genuinely are, not where you would like to be. A low score on any component is not a judgement. It is a starting point.

Think about your actual behaviour in the last few weeks. Think about specific moments and real situations. Score what you do, not what you intend to do.

The leaders who get the most from this are the ones who are willing to be honest with themselves. That honesty is the first act of authentic leadership.

The 11 Components of Authentic Leadership with a Collective Conscience
Hover over any component to see what it means. The first seven build the leader. The final four are what that leader does with what they have built.
Individual LayerHow you show up as a leader
01Your values show up in your decisions, especially when it would be easier to look the other way.Integrity
02What you do carries more weight than what you say. Your behaviour sets the standard.Lead by Example
03You connect people to a purpose bigger than the task, and they move because they believe, not because they were told.Inspire and Influence
04You invest in real relationships with your people, not just professional ones.Model Connection
05When things are uncertain, you help people find their footing rather than pretending you have all the answers.Clarity in Complexity
06You stay genuinely open to changing how you lead, not just what you do.Adapt and Evolve
07You develop people to make decisions and grow without needing you as the bottleneck.Empower Others
Collective LayerHow you lead beyond yourself
08Your team knows what they are building together and why it matters beyond the immediate task.Shared Purpose
09Your sense of direction is visible in the decisions and priorities you set every week.Vision into Action
10You bring people together across boundaries and share credit generously.Collaborate
11You lead in a way that creates value beyond your team, your targets, and yourself.Serve the Greater Good
