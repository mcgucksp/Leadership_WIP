What Drives Me as a Leader?
Before you begin, here is what this assessment explores. There are three leadership motivation types. Most leaders have a dominant one, with elements of the others. Understanding yours helps you lead with more self-awareness and intention.
The Driver
Achievement & Results
Motivated by challenge, goals and getting things done. Energised by progress and driven by high standards.
The Connector
Relationships & Belonging
Motivated by people, harmony and trust. Energised by building strong relationships and creating environments where others thrive.
The Influencer
Impact & Recognition
Motivated by making a difference at scale. Energised by having a platform, leading others and driving meaningful change.
A note before you start: There are no right or wrong answers and no better or worse motivation type. Answer honestly — your first instinct is usually the most revealing. The assessment has 21 statements across 3 short pages.
