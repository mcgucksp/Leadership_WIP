How Curious Am I?

Curiosity is not a personality trait you either have or do not have. It is a leadership discipline: the choice to approach situations with genuine openness rather than pre-formed judgement, to stay interested in what you do not yet know, and to treat not knowing as a starting point rather than a weakness. This assessment explores five dimensions of curiosity that together determine whether you are leading from genuine inquiry or from the comfort of conclusions you have already reached.
